Frequently asked

At the Saratoga Springs Utah Temple. Check with your leaders if your family is carpooling or meeting at the chapel first.

Plan to be at the temple by 6:00 AM so we can start promptly at 6:15.

Bring your temple recommend (or limited-use recommend for youth) and a change of clothes if you're heading to school afterward. The temple provides everything you need for the ordinance.

Sunday dress to and from the temple. Modest, respectful clothing. You'll change into white baptism clothing provided by the temple.

Yes — a current limited-use recommend (for youth) or full recommend is required to enter the temple. If yours has expired, please contact your bishopric to renew before the day.

Plan on being at the temple for about an hour. You should be heading home (or to school) by 7:30 AM.
