Description
Legacy Campground is centrally located alongside Main Creek. The pavilion covers 16 picnic tables, a food preparation area with a kitchen, storage shelves, and two refrigerators. There is a hot water sink available along with two large gas grills, a double burner stove, and a Dutch oven table. Propane is provided for our equipment. There are three bathrooms with a total of five stalls and three tiled hot water shower stations. The pavilion is close to parking, the ball field, sand volleyball, a large amphitheater, and a grass area for activities. All camps have a picnic table and 4-5 leveled tent sites designed for 12‘x15' group tents. Parking for RV trailers is limited but there is one with full hookups and 30 amp power. There is a large concrete fire-pit by the pavilion and each camp has its own fire-pit. Two camp sites have a canvas pavilion.

No Trailer parking we are a tent camp if you need to bring a trailer it needs to be pre approved, nothing over 26 ft and it will be dry camp NO HOOK UPS

Frequently asked

Monday July 20 through Thursday July 23, 2026 at the Wallsburg Recreation Camp (Legacy Campground) — 2474 E Main Canyon Rd, Wallsburg, UT 84082. The camp is 4 miles east of the Wallsburg City stop sign on Main Canyon Road.

A covered pavilion with 16 picnic tables and a kitchen area (hot-water sink, two refrigerators, storage shelves), two large gas grills, a double burner stove, and a Dutch oven table with propane. Three bathrooms with five stalls and three hot showers. A concrete fire pit at the pavilion, plus individual fire pits at each camp site. Two camp sites also have a canvas pavilion.

This is a tent camp — no trailer parking by default. If you need to bring one it must be pre-approved, nothing longer than 26 ft, and it will be a dry camp with no hook-ups. One RV site with full hookups (30 amp) exists but is limited.

Tent, sleeping bag/pad, camp chair, personal toiletries, sunscreen, water bottle, flashlight, closed-toe shoes for the trails, and clothes for creek/water play. Coordinate with your family camp leader on shared meals and shared gear.

Ball field, sand volleyball court, a large amphitheater, and a grass area for group games. Main Creek runs alongside the camp for water play and cooling off.

Camp Manager Jason Newsom — (801) 837-8034. Camp Scheduler Kathleen Tibbs — (801) 367-5025. General camp email — WallsburgCamp@churchservicemail.org. For ward-specific logistics, reach out to your ward camp director.

Use the map link on this page — it opens the exact pin in Google Maps. Wallsburg is about a 45-minute drive from Lehi via I-15 south to US-40 through Heber.
